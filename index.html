Telangana Festivals & Events

Experience the vibrant spirit through unique festivals

🌸

Bathukamma Festival

Colourful flower festival celebrating spring and prosperity. Women create floral towers and dance in vibrant traditional attire.

📅 September–October📍 State-wide
🐄

Bonalu Festival

Harvest festival with grand processions and offerings to goddesses throughout Hyderabad and Secunderabad.

📅 July–August📍 Hyderabad
🏯

Hyderabad Shourya

Grand cultural showcase blending traditional Telangana dance, folk music, and contemporary art.

📅 February📍 Hyderabad City
🎭

Medaram Jatara

World's largest tribal festival held every two years in Eturnagaram forests — over a million devotees.

📅 February (biennial)📍 Medaram, Mulugu
